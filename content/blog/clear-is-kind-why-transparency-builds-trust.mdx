> “Clear is kind.” — Brené Brown

It’s one of our guiding principles at Brik, and we think it’s worth talking about.

When you’re running a business, your time is everything. And yet, so many creative studios still ask you to *inquire* before you even know if something fits your budget.

We’ve been on the other side of that—and it’s frustrating.

So we decided to do things differently.

At Brik, all of our services and pricing are published publicly. No gatekeeping. No mystery discovery calls. Just full transparency from the start.

Because clarity isn’t just efficient—it’s *empowering.*

---

### **Why “Clear is Kind” Matters in Design**

When clients know what to expect—how we work, what it costs, how long it takes—it sets the tone for the entire relationship.

There’s less anxiety.

There’s more trust.

And that trust leads to better work.

This kind of clarity also protects *us* as designers. It keeps scope clean, communication open, and projects aligned from day one.

---

### **Transparency Isn’t Just Pricing—It’s Experience Design**

Being clear is also about how we structure our proposals, how we write emails, how we hand off files.

Because design is about more than visuals. It’s about how someone experiences working with you.

And in a cluttered, salesy world, sometimes the best experience you can offer is *honesty.*
