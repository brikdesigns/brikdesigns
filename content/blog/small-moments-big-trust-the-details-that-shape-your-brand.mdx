We often think about trust as something you earn over time.

But the truth is, trust is built—or broken—in small, everyday moments:

- The clarity of your proposals
- The tone of your emails
- The ease of your onboarding process
- The consistency of your visuals

Every detail is a chance to show that you’re reliable, thoughtful, and human. [Clear, upfront communication](/blog/clear-is-kind-why-transparency-builds-trust) is one of the most reliable ways to build trust.

---

### **A few questions to ask yourself this month:**

- Is my process clear from the start?
- Do my touch points feel cohesive and consistent?
- Where could I add more clarity or kindness?

‍

Because [design isn’t just about how something looks](/blog/design-isnt-a-luxury-its-essential).

It’s about how it feels to work with you.

And the little things? They’re often the big things. Created moment by moment.
